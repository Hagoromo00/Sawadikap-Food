SAWADIKAP


SAWADIKAP TRADEMARK FOOD DIVERSITY IN SOUTHEAST ASIA is a website featuring foods from countries in Southeast Asia. This website is intended for tourists to know about Southeast Asian Culinary and the ingredients of these foods.

This website was created to inform tourists about typical foods from countries in Southeast Asia. This website is also equipped with food ingredients used to make these foods. The goal is to help users avoid ingredients that are incompatible with their bodies.
